# Enterprise Salesforce Flow Knowledge Pack

## Purpose

The Enterprise Salesforce Flow Knowledge Pack defines enterprise-approved Flow governance rules for Salesforce product teams. It extends the Enterprise Spec Framework with Flow-specific controls for design, implementation planning, review, testing, activation, and operations.

## Scope

This pack covers Screen Flow, Record-Triggered Flow, Scheduled Flow, error handling, performance, fault paths, subflows, naming, versioning, reuse, testing, and governance.

## Rule Location

Rules are stored in:

```text
enterprise/rules/salesforce-flow/
```

Rule IDs use the `SFFLOW-` prefix.

## Ownership

Primary owner: Salesforce Center of Excellence

Contributors:

- Business Process Owners
- Salesforce Administrators
- Platform Engineering
- Quality Engineering
- Enterprise Architecture

## Lifecycle

Rules are advisory, warning, or blocking according to delivery risk. Product teams may request exceptions through the enterprise exception process, but rule IDs are stable and must not be reused after retirement.
