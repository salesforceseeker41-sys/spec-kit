# Compliance Principles

## Purpose

Describe compliance expectations for regulated data, auditability, retention, and operational controls.

## Ownership

Owned by the Platform Team with input from compliance and security stakeholders.

## Starter Guidance

- Identify sensitive data and PII in every feature that stores or transmits customer data.
- Describe audit trail requirements for material business events.
- Follow enterprise retention and deletion expectations.
- Include compliance impact in specifications and plans.
- Prefer configurable controls over hardcoded compliance behavior.
