# Governance Principles

## Purpose

Define how architecture decisions, standards, and delivery evidence are managed across Salesforce projects.

## Ownership

Owned by the Platform Team. Product Teams may add product-specific governance in `products/<product>/`.

## Starter Guidance

- Keep enterprise standards centralized.
- Keep product rules close to the product domain.
- Keep feature decisions in feature artifacts.
- Treat governance findings as recommendations until blocking validation is explicitly enabled.
- Record exceptions with rationale, owner, and review date.
