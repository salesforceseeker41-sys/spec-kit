# Apex Standards

## Purpose

Define Apex implementation standards for secure, scalable, and testable Salesforce delivery.

## Ownership

Owned by the Platform Team.

## Starter Guidance

- Use bulkified services and trigger handlers.
- Avoid SOQL, SOSL, and DML inside loops.
- Enforce CRUD/FLS and sharing requirements.
- Use meaningful exception handling and structured logging.
- Include positive, negative, bulk, and permission-related tests.
