# Sample Product Principles

## Purpose

Capture product-owned business principles for this Salesforce application.

## Ownership

Owned by the Product Team. Replace `sample-product` with your product identifier when adopting this scaffold.

## Starter Guidance

- Define the product mission and primary users.
- Capture product-specific business rules.
- Identify product-level non-functional expectations.
- Reference enterprise standards rather than copying them.
- Keep delivery teams focused on feature requirements under `specs/`.
