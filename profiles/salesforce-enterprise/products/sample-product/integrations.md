# Sample Product Integrations

## Purpose

Document systems, APIs, data contracts, and operational expectations for this product.

## Ownership

Owned by the Product Team with support from integration owners.

## Starter Guidance

- List upstream and downstream systems.
- Document authentication and Named Credential expectations.
- Identify retry, timeout, and idempotency requirements.
- Capture ownership and support contacts.
- Link to formal API contracts where available.
