# Sample Product Events

## Purpose

Document domain events and integration events used by this product.

## Ownership

Owned by the Product Team with support from architecture and integration stakeholders.

## Starter Guidance

- Define event names, producers, and consumers.
- Capture payload ownership and versioning expectations.
- Document replay, ordering, and idempotency requirements.
- Identify event monitoring and support expectations.
- Link events to business outcomes.
